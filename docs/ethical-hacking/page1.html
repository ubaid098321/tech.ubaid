Introduction to Ethical Hacking

Welcome to the comprehensive Ethical Hacking course by tech.ubaid. This course is designed to take you from a complete beginner to an advanced ethical hacker, step by step.

What is Ethical Hacking?

Ethical hacking is the authorized practice of bypassing system security to identify potential data breaches and threats in a network. It helps organizations strengthen their security measures.

Why Learn Ethical Hacking?

  • High demand career with good salary.
  • Understand how hackers think and operate.
  • Protect yourself and others from cyber threats.
  • Help organizations secure their systems.
