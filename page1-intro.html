🔐 Introduction to Ethical Hacking

🌟 What is Ethical Hacking?

Ethical hacking means testing and breaking into computer systems, but with permission, to find and fix security weaknesses. It’s also called White Hat hacking.

🧠 Why is Ethical Hacking Important?

  • 🛡️ Protects data and systems from real hackers
  • 🔍 Helps companies discover security flaws
  • 💼 Creates career opportunities in cyber security
  • 🧑‍💻 Builds strong understanding of how systems work

⚔️ Types of Hackers

TypeDescription
👨‍💻 White HatEthical hacker (good guy)
🕵️ Black HatMalicious hacker (bad guy)
🧥 Grey HatSometimes good, sometimes bad

🔧 Skills You Need to Start

  • Basic knowledge of computers & internet
  • Understanding of operating systems (especially Linux)
  • Familiarity with networking
  • Willingness to learn tools like Termux, Nmap, Metasploit
